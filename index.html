A Brief Introduction



Developer

I have been studying development since I was first introduced to PHP in highschool. Since then, I have fallen in love with Java and have been actively practicing my web and software development with both schooling and personal projects.

Musician

At age 14 I picked up my first pair of drumsticks. At age 15, it was guitar. Then it was bass, piano, violin, ukelale, and so on. I've dedicated so much of my time to music it stopped me from studying law. I've released one record so far, and hoping to up my game soon.

Photographer

Thank you Peter Mckinnon for making me rapidly become obsessed with photography... I'm pretty new to this world, but I got my first DSLR as a 21st birthday gift and have used it every second I can. Hoping one day to distinguish myself and set my own style.

Tester

My almost career! I studied testing throughout university and nearly chose this field over development. Overall, I still quite enjoy testing, and I have experience in manual testing, unit testing, and automation testing.

Graphic Designer

I got into this as a means of designing my album covers for music I would never release... Since then, I've applied these skills to creating posters for various clients on a casual basis, including promotional material for my former job at a franchise pawn shop.

Tech Enthusiast

My entire family works in IT, so I've been exposed to technology all my life. Laptops, Computers, Tablets, Phones, Earbuds, Headphones, I know it all!
